Work

As a Master’s student in Computer Science at Rochester Institute of Technology, I have built a solid foundation in software development and problem-solving. My coursework in Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) with Java and Problem Solving with Python has sharpened my coding skills, while my studies in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning have deepened my understanding of their practical applications.

In my professional experience as an Associate Systems Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services, I spent two years developing customized SharePoint team sites using the SharePoint framework and JavaScript. I also built applications with Power Apps to address internal team needs and leveraged workflow automation tools like Power Automate. My role exposed me to cloud technologies such as Azure, AWS, and GCP, where I completed several certifications to enhance my expertise.

Feel free to explore my projects and contributions on GitHub or connect with me on LinkedIn.

Projects

Mobile CRM Application
